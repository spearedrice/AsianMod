
# asianmod

![son](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/spearedrice/AsianMod/refs/heads/master/thumb.png)

a fabric expansion mod that adds traditional building materials, unique items, and some very specific survival challenges to your game.

---

## how to install

* **loader:** built for the fabric mod loader. make sure you have fabric api installed.
* **game version:** made for mounts of mayhem (1.21.11). it will not work on newer minecraft versions and may break or crash.
* **multiplayer:** must be installed on both server and client or things will break.
* **singleplayer:** just drop it in your mods folder.

---

## what’s in the mod

### gear & weapons

* **nephrite jade gear:** full tool and armor set. stats are around iron level, but it has a hidden perk: extremely high enchantability (basically gold tier), so you can get strong enchant combos easily.
* **the slipper:** stackable throwable weapon for ranged attacks.
* **flying needle:** precise utility weapon, max stack size of 3.
* **new enchantments:**

    * thundering
    * curse of repulsion
    * they don't work
---

### mobs & items

* **asian dad mob:** hostile overworld mob inspired by steven he’s “asian dad” character from his comedy videos. not affiliated with or endorsed by steven he. custom ai and loot.
* **rice wine:** drink that gives status effects. can be stored in a bucket.
* **mercury:** liquid block you can pick up with a bucket.
* **fugu:** food item that might poison you.
* **lanterns:** sky lanterns and paper lanterns you can place or release.

---

### blocks & building

* **porcelain set:** logs, leaves, blocks, stairs, slabs, fences.
* **traditional materials:** rammed earth, compacted timber, clay pipes, lingzhi, steel blocks, vertical bamboo slabs.
* **utility blocks:**

    * bellows block (interactive, sound effects)
    * brass chest (storage)
    * oil lamp (light source)
    * abacus (functional decorative block)
* **nephrite door:** heavy stone-tier door.
